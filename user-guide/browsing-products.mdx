# Browsing Products

The Sabbels Handmade product catalog makes it easy to discover unique, handcrafted items. Use powerful search and filtering tools to find exactly what you're looking for.

## Accessing the Catalog

Navigate to the product catalog from:
- The main navigation menu ("Products" or "Shop" link)
- The home page "Browse Collection" button
- Direct URL: `/items`

## Product Display

Products are displayed in a responsive grid layout showing:
- Product image
- Product name
- Price (starting from base price)
- Category path (e.g., "Accessories › Bags")
- Average rating and review count (if available)

<Info>
  The product grid automatically adjusts based on your screen size for optimal viewing on mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.
</Info>

## Search Functionality

### Using the Search Bar

The search bar is located in the top-right of the product list page.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter search term">
    Type your search query into the search box. The search is case-insensitive.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View results">
    Results update automatically as you type. The search looks through:
    - Product names
    - Product descriptions
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear search">
    Delete your search term or use the "Clear All" button to reset filters.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Search Tips

- Search is real-time - results update as you type
- Partial matches are supported (e.g., "bag" will find "Tote Bag")
- Search queries are preserved in the URL, so you can bookmark or share searches

## Filtering Products

### Category Filter

Filter products by category to narrow down your search:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open filters panel">
    On mobile: Tap the "Filters" button at the top of the product list
    
    On desktop: The filters sidebar is always visible on the left side
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a category">
    Click on any category name to filter by that category. Categories are organized hierarchically:
    - Parent categories (e.g., "Accessories")
    - Subcategories (e.g., "Accessories › Bags")
  </Step>
  <Step title="View filtered results">
    Products are automatically filtered to show only items in the selected category and all its subcategories
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  When you select a parent category, products from all subcategories are included in the results.
</Note>

### Price Range Filter

Filter products by price to match your budget:

1. In the filters panel, locate the "Price" section
2. Enter a minimum price, maximum price, or both
3. Products are filtered to show items within your specified range
4. The filter considers both base prices and variant prices

<Tip>
  Leave one field empty to set only a minimum (e.g., "€20 and up") or maximum (e.g., "under €50") price.
</Tip>

### Clearing Filters

To reset all filters:
- Click the "Reset" or "Clear All" button at the bottom of the filters panel
- This clears category selection and price range filters
- Search terms are also cleared

## Sorting Products

Use the sort dropdown (top-right of the product list) to organize results:

| Sort Option | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| **Name** | Alphabetical order (A-Z) |
| **Price: Low to High** | Cheapest items first |
| **Price: High to Low** | Most expensive items first |
| **Newest** | Recently added items first |
| **Top Rated** | Highest rated items first (by average rating, then review count) |

<Note>
  The default sort order is by name (alphabetical).
</Note>

## Viewing Product Details

Click on any product card to view full details including:
- High-resolution images with gallery view
- Complete product description
- Available sizes and colors
- Customization options (hook types)
- Customer reviews and ratings
- Related products

See [Product Details](/user-guide/browsing-products#product-page) for more information.

## URL Parameters

Filters and search are reflected in the URL, allowing you to:
- Bookmark specific searches or filters
- Share product searches with others
- Navigate back to previous searches using browser history

Example URL: `/items?search=bag&category=accessories&minPrice=20&maxPrice=50&sort=price`

## No Results

If your search or filters return no results:
- You'll see a "No products found" message
- Click the "Clear All" button to reset filters and start over
- Try broader search terms or remove some filters

<Tip>
  The product count (e.g., "24 products") is always displayed at the top of the list to show how many items match your current filters.
</Tip>

## Mobile Experience

On mobile devices:
- Filters are hidden by default to maximize screen space
- Tap the "Filters" button to open the filters panel
- The panel slides in from the side with a backdrop overlay
- Close the panel by tapping the X button or the backdrop
- All filtering functionality is identical to the desktop experience
