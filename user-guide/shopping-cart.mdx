# Shopping Cart

The shopping cart allows you to collect items before proceeding to checkout. You can manage quantities, review selections, and see your order total.

## Adding Items to Cart

### From Product Detail Page

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select product options">
    On the product detail page, choose:
    - **Size**: Select from available sizes (or "One Size" if no variants)
    - **Color**: Pick a color from the color picker (if available)
    - **Hook Type**: Choose your preferred crochet hook pattern:
      - Default (standard pattern)
      - Rechtsmuster (right-handed pattern)
      - Gänsefüßchen (special decorative pattern)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set quantity">
    Use the quantity controls:
    - Click **-** to decrease quantity
    - Click **+** to increase quantity
    - Type directly into the quantity input
    - Quantity is limited by available stock
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add to cart">
    Click the **"Add to Cart"** button. You'll see:
    - A success notification confirming the item was added
    - The notification disappears after 3 seconds
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The "Add to Cart" button is disabled if:
  - The selected variant is out of stock
  - No variant is selected
  - The page is still loading
</Note>

### Product Variants

Each cart item represents a unique combination of:
- Base product
- Selected size
- Selected color
- Hook type preference

Adding the same product with different options creates separate cart items.

## Viewing Your Cart

Access your cart by:
- Clicking the cart icon in the navigation bar
- Navigating to `/cart` directly

### Cart Page Layout

The cart page shows:

**Left Section (Cart Items)**
- List of all items in your cart
- Item image, name, and specifications
- Quantity controls
- Individual and total prices
- Remove button

**Right Section (Order Summary)**
- Subtotal
- Shipping information
- Total amount
- Checkout button or login prompt

## Managing Cart Items

### Updating Quantities

For each item in your cart:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Decrease quantity">
    Click the **-** button to reduce quantity by 1. If quantity reaches 0, the item is removed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Increase quantity">
    Click the **+** button to add more. The button is disabled if you've reached available stock.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  The price display shows both unit price and total price (unit price × quantity) for each item.
</Info>

### Removing Items

To remove an item from your cart:

**On Desktop:**
- Click the trash icon button on the right side of the cart item

**On Mobile:**
- Tap the trash icon in the item header (top-right of item card)

<Tip>
  The item is removed immediately. If you remove an item by accident, you'll need to add it again from the product page.
</Tip>

## Cart Item Details

Each cart item displays:

| Information | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| **Image** | Product thumbnail |
| **Name** | Product name |
| **Size** | Selected size variant |
| **Color** | Selected color name |
| **Hook Type** | Chosen crochet pattern (if applicable) |
| **Unit Price** | Price per item |
| **Quantity** | Number of items |
| **Total** | Unit price × quantity |

## Order Summary

The order summary sidebar shows:

### Subtotal
Sum of all items in cart (quantity × unit price for each item)

### Shipping
Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on:
- Delivery address
- Order total
- Shipping method

<Note>
  Shipping costs are not included in the cart subtotal. They will be shown during the checkout process.
</Note>

### Total
Current cart subtotal (shipping will be added at checkout)

## Cart Features

### Item Count Display

At the top of the cart page, you'll see the total number of items:
- Example: "5 articles" (total of all quantities across all cart items)

### Trust Badges

Below the cart items, you'll find information about:
- **Fast Shipping**: Delivery within 24-48 hours
- **Secure Payment**: SSL encrypted transactions

Click these badges to learn more in the FAQ section.

## Empty Cart

If your cart is empty, you'll see:
- A shopping bag icon
- "Your cart is empty" message
- A button to browse the product catalog

## Proceeding to Checkout

### If Logged In

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review your cart">
    Verify all items, quantities, and options are correct
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click checkout button">
    The **"Proceed to Checkout"** button is located in the order summary sidebar
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete payment">
    You'll be redirected to the checkout page to enter shipping and payment information
  </Step>
</Steps>

### If Not Logged In

You must log in before checking out:

1. You'll see a login prompt in the order summary section
2. Click the **"Log In"** button
3. After logging in, you'll return to your cart
4. Your cart items are preserved across login

<Info>
  Creating an account is required to complete a purchase. This allows you to track your order and view order history.
</Info>

## Continue Shopping

To add more items:
- Click **"Continue Shopping"** at the bottom of the order summary
- You'll return to the product catalog
- Your cart is preserved while you browse

## Cart Persistence

Your cart:
- Is stored in local browser storage
- Persists across browser sessions
- Is maintained when you log in/out
- Is cleared only when you:
  - Manually remove all items
  - Complete a purchase
  - Clear your browser data

<Tip>
  You can safely close your browser and return later - your cart will still be there!
</Tip>

## Stock Limitations

If an item has limited stock:
- The **+** button is disabled when you reach the maximum available quantity
- You cannot add more than the available stock to your cart
- Stock levels are checked in real-time when you add items

## Mobile Experience

On mobile devices:
- Cart items display in a single column
- Item details are stacked vertically for easier reading
- Remove button appears in the item header
- Order summary is positioned below the cart items
- All functionality remains the same as desktop
